# Flousi — Visual Reference Library

A curated catalog of UI/UX design references collected for the **Flousi** project
(a money / crypto / finance product). These screenshots were uploaded to the repo
on 2026-06-25 and are treated as **visual feedback / design fuel** — use them as
inspiration for layout, components, color, motion, and overall taste during any
design or frontend work.

> How to use: when building a screen or component, scan the matching category below,
> open the referenced image, and borrow the patterns (not pixel-copy). Pair this with
> the design toolkit at the bottom of this file.

## Cross-cutting visual themes observed

- **Two dominant modes:** clean light neumorphic/soft-UI (finance core flows, steppers, plan/radio selectors) and premium dark + glow (crypto analytics, CTAs, illustrations).
- **Accents:** blue (primary/finance), green (success/crypto positive), red (error/alert), purple & magenta (feature sections), orange (badges).
- **Recurring devices:** grainy mesh gradients (grainient), glossy 3D radio orbs, neumorphic widgets, pill nav/segmented controls, KPI strips, bento grids, connected step indicators, success/error state pairs.
- **Typography:** bold/condensed headlines, monospace for data/crypto contexts, serif accents for wellness.
